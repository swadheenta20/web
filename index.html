About Swadheenta




Meaning Self-Dependence in Hindi, Swadheenta invites developers to start with ideas, code them into prototypes and grill them through expert sessions into business solutions. Today, the world needs, not pieces of code, but solutions, and Swadheenta aims to provide that in 48 hours!!


That's not all!! We are a movement, not just an event. With a dizzying array of project presentations, workshops, and sessions, we aim to go Swadheen!!

Hackathon Indradhanush Workshops

Hack Infinity 2.0




One of India's largest student hackathons, Hack Infinity 2.0 presents pocket-watering prizes, an undeniable thrill and a huge knowledge base. Hack Infinity 2.0 gains its inspiration from Hack Infinity 2017, which was a grand success. A software only hackathon, Hack Infinity 2.0 brings together developers to compete on some of the most industry relevant topics of today.

FAQs

At Swadheenta, we strive to bring together developers to participate in the hackathon irrespective of gender, race, sexual orientation, socioeconomic background or ethnicity. We usually try to keep things simple. Read our FAQs to phase out ambiguity.

Who can participate?
Students presently enrolled at a recognised university, aged 16-28 years.
Any hardware involvement?
As of this date, none. We work on pure code.
Travel Reimbursements
Yes!!! Provided you submit all details, you will be reimbursed. Of course, there's a cap.
Do I have to pay anything?
Na na na.... You just have to come and code. We take care of your food, travel, drinks, bathrooms(if you are into bathing at all).
