About Indradhanush




A 16 hours business hackathon to be organized in association with startup india following are the features.

  1. Team formation.
  2. Mentor inspired workshops on-
    • Business model formation.
    • Investor planning

    • Brand Development
  3. Mock-pitching and proposal refinement

Attention : Startup enthusiasts have a chance to receive long term mentorship from the best venture capitalists, entrepreneur of the country.

Women in Entrepreneurship Program

Indradhanush Entrepreneurship Workshop is being organized as a part of the Women in Entrepreneurship Program initiated by DCEI.
The WEP, by aid of various workshops, aims to promote and garner support for women entrepreneurs.
