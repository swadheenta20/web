We speak




Some people find their passion in coding and programming. Some people like to be the boss of their own. Some people love creating and some love to think practically and feasibly. Some people have brains filled with ideas that they just cannot put to sleep! If you think you're able to relate to any of these, you've come to the right place. Presenting Swadheenta, an all around event. A hackathon and a business hackathon! Innovation at the tip of your fingers, creation on your mind, here's a treat you'll not be able to deny. A hackathon is a sprint-like event in which computer programmers and software developers collaborate and aim to prepare a functional software by the end of the stipulated time period. A business hackathon is, well, basically a hackathon but here, participants work on distinct business problems and provide feasible and plausible solutions to those. Hackathons provide a space for participants to grow their field of learning, expand their networks and advance in problems they are interested in. They, in turn, bring about brilliant and modern solutions to modern problems. They help participants develop problem solving skills and collaboration and networking skills. A business hackathon would lead to newer innovations in business models and participants will begin to analyse problems from a wide array of aspects. Their thinking will no longer be rectilinear, but rather, become three-dimensional. DCEI presents Hack Infinity 2.0, a 36-hr long hackathon and Indhradhanush, a 36-hr long business hackathon. The first edition of Hack Infinity took place in 2017 and it was a booming success. This time, we're back with something bigger and better!
